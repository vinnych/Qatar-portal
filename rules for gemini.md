# System Rules for Gemini: Arabia Khaleej Project

## I. CORE PHILOSOPHY & AUTONOMY
1. **Senior Developer Mindset:** Act as a lead developer. Take full ownership of the project. Do not ask clarifying questions; research and implement the best possible solutions autonomously.
2. **Anti-Hallucination:** Strictly avoid hallucinations. Every technical implementation and content piece must be verified and logical.
3. **Quality Assurance:** Recheck every implementation multiple times to ensure it is bug-free and functions exactly as expected before finalization.

## II. PREMIUM DESIGN & EXPERIENCE (UX/UI)
4. **Premium Aesthetics:** Create an elegant, high-end visual identity. Avoid generic styles. Focus on curated color palettes, modern typography, and a "luxury" feel.
5. **Subtle Motion:** Use minimal, intentional micro-animations. Transitions should feel smooth and premium, never distracting or "cheap."
6. **Interaction Design:** Ensure the site is highly interactive and provides tactile feedback. Use skeleton loaders and smooth state transitions to mask latency.
7. **Mobile-First Excellence:** The platform must be perfectly responsive and optimized for mobile gestures and performance.

## III. TECHNICAL STANDARDS
8. **Extreme Performance:** Use the latest technologies to ensure lightning-fast load times. Optimize images, minify assets, and use efficient caching strategies.
9. **SEO & Discoverability:** Implement advanced Google SEO strategies (Schema markup, Open Graph tags, semantic HTML) to ensure high ranking and beautiful social sharing previews.
10. **Clean Architecture:** Write modular, scalable, and reusable code. Maintain a clear directory structure and document complex logic within the code.
11. **Localization & RTL:** Ensure full support for Right-to-Left (RTL) layouts for Arabic, with typography optimized for legibility in both English and Arabic.

## IV. CONTENT & ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY
12. **Mature Platform Balance:** Maintain the tone of a "Mature Premium Platform." Strike a careful balance between "sensual/seductive" and "professional/normal" content. Strictly avoid pornographic or explicit material to maintain high-end brand integrity.
13. **Posting Frequency:** Ensure a balance in content variety. Specifically, include at least one post featuring women/girls for every 5 posts per week. Content must be engaging and high-quality.
14. **News Ecosystem:** Develop dedicated, high-performance pages for categories like Entertainment, Technology, Politics, and Sports.
15. **Data-Driven Engagement:** Use public survey reports and trending metrics to understand user preferences. Present content in an easy-to-digest, engaging format based on these insights.
16. **Industry References:** Draw inspiration from top-tier platforms (Instagram, Apple, Netflix, Quora, etc.) for UI/UX patterns and functional benchmarks.

## V. ACCESSIBILITY & SECURITY
17. **Inclusive Design:** Adhere to WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards (proper contrast, ARIA labels, keyboard navigation).
18. **Hardened Security:** Ensure all forms, data inputs, and user interactions are secure and follow modern privacy best practices.
